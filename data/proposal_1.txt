
XYZ Cloud Solutions Pvt Ltd

CLIENT PROPOSAL

Project:
AI-Powered Patient Engagement and Appointment Automation Platform

Client Industry:
Healthcare / Hospitals

Client Requirement:
The client is a multi-specialty hospital managing more than 5000 monthly patient interactions.

The hospital currently handles appointment booking, patient queries, and follow-up communication manually through call centers.

Challenges:
1. Long patient waiting time during peak hours.
2. High workload on reception staff.
3. Missed follow-up appointments.
4. No centralized patient communication history.

Business Objective:
Develop an AI-based patient engagement platform that improves appointment management and reduces administrative workload.

Proposed Solution:
XYZ Cloud Solutions proposes an AI healthcare assistant capable of:

- Automated appointment scheduling
- Patient query resolution
- Appointment reminders
- Follow-up notifications
- Patient history management

Technical Approach:
Frontend:
React.js

Backend:
Python FastAPI

AI Layer:
Large Language Model based conversational assistant

Database:
PostgreSQL

Cloud:
AWS

Implementation Scope:
Phase 1:
Requirement analysis and workflow mapping

Phase 2:
AI chatbot development

Phase 3:
Hospital management system integration

Phase 4:
Testing and deployment

Timeline:
Estimated Duration: 16 Weeks

Deliverables:
- AI Patient Assistant
- Appointment automation module
- Admin dashboard
- Deployment documentation

Expected Business Impact:
- 40% reduction in manual appointment calls
- Faster patient response time
- Improved patient experience

Estimated Investment:

Development Cost:
$45,000

Maintenance:
$5,000/year

Assumptions:
Hospital will provide required APIs for existing healthcare systems.
